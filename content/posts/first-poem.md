The evening stretches out,
a canvas of bruised purple
and fading ember gold.

I stand beneath it and wonder—
how does the sky hold so much quiet,
while the earth beneath it
is so loud?

It asks for nothing,
gives no answers,
just remains,
vast
and unbroken.

And for a moment,
I am unbroken, too.
